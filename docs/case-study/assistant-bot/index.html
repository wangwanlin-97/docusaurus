Case Study: Assistant BOT

随着社会节奏的不断变快，每天，我们都有越来越多的微信消息需要回复，其中不乏很多重复的问题反复找到我。 作为一个程序员，都是不愿意在重复的工作中浪费自己宝贵的时间的。

这里，将给大家分享如何用 Wechaty 和 OpenAI 创建一个属于你的个人助理， 帮助你来处理那些重复的问题，解放你的时间。

我们要做什么？​

一个微信助理机器人带有以下功能：

  • 我@他，并且提问的时候，自动把准备好的材料等都发出来
  • 别人问他的时候，可以自动匹配到常见问题并回答
  • 当没有匹配到回答的时候，转接给我
    • 消息在群里且我在群里：@我让我回答
    • 私聊或者我不在群里：把我的名片发出来，引导加我好友

If you understand English, this section is for you!​

As the pace of society keeps getting faster, every day, we have more and more WeChat messages that need to be answered, and there are many repetitive questions that repeatedly find me. As a programmer, you are unwilling to waste your precious time in repetitive work.

Here, I will share with you how to use Wechaty and OpenAI to create a personal assistant that belongs to you. Help you deal with those repetitive problems and free up your time.

What are we going to do?​

A WeChat assistant robot has the following functions:

-I @他, and when I ask questions, I automatically send out all the prepared materials -When others ask him, it can automatically match common questions and answer them -When no answer is matched, transfer it to me -The message is in the group and I am in the group: @我 let me answer -Private chat or I am not in the group: send out my business card and guide to add me as a friend
